RETAIL SALES ANALYTICS — BUSINESS INSIGHTS

1. Technology category generated the highest sales revenue.

2. Western region contributed the highest overall profit.

3. Some furniture sub-categories produced losses despite strong sales.

4. A small group of customers contributed a large portion of total revenue.

5. Monthly sales trends showed fluctuations with growth toward later years.

6. Standard Class shipping required the highest average delivery time.

7. Loss-making products may be impacted by discounts, operational costs, or low margins.

8. Customer behavior analysis showed repeat high-value customers.

RECOMMENDATIONS

• Focus marketing efforts on profitable product categories.
• Reduce discounts on consistently loss-making products.
• Improve shipping efficiency for slower delivery methods.
• Create loyalty programs for top customers.
